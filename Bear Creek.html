Bear Creek

Bear Creek

This piece was inspired by my first time fly fishing. Bear Creek was the site of a trout stream restoration project, which improved fish habitat and ecology in order to sustain trout populations. It is the first place I expereince both fly fishig and catching trout, and I wanted to commemorate that through mapping the resortation effort that made it possible.
