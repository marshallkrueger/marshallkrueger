Hello.

I'm Marshall.


I'm a geographer and a designer.

I have a Bachelor's in Cartography, GIS, and Human Geography from the University of Wisconsin - Madison.
I specialize in helping others use and understand their spatial data. I also focus on media accessibility through my design.
I have 3 years of experience working in educational media, where I've developed a strong sense of user needs, design techniques, and multimedia management.
My other interests include feminist cartography, environmental equity, urban studies, and web development.
