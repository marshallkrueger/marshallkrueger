State Control

A Mapbox Studio Design

State Control

This project was inspired by the Summer Black Lives Matter protests in Madison, WI. In response to protests, the police and sate exerted control over the citizens of Madison, including unjustified use of force against protestors and a curfew that limited citizens from leaving their houses after 9PM, regardless of involvement in protesting. I wanted to explore the use of cartography in highlighting the spatial locations of state power. This map is intended for use as a template for those who would like to avoid or document the presence of police and other state actors in either their political movements or day to day lives. Design inspiration comes from Theo Deutinger’s “Crowd Control” in Handbook of Tyranny.
